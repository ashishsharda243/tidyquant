Credit Card Fraud Detection















Credit Card Fraud Detection

It has indeed become an Achilles heel for banks all across the globe to check on frequent instances of fraudulent uses of credit/debit card. Any guideline or precautionary measure introduced are more of a reactive approach, something fraudsters always find a way around while trying their tricks next time around. The need of the hour is a proactive solution that sends out explicit signals to bank to avoid any such instances to occur. The credit card fraud detection solution developed by Tidyquant works precisely as a proactive measure. The credit card fraud detection solution considers previous credit card transactions, patterns of usage and few other factors on basis of which a solution is modeled that immediately identifies any irregularity in usage or unobserved pattern and sends out alerts that allows banks to inform their customers who in turn can take precautionary measures rather than going through financial and mental trauma.

Banks always have in their possession silos of data about their customers’ credit card/debit card usage for an entire customer journey. This data is then used to perform data analysis and draw insights to identify any unusual and out of line patterns to determine any probabilities that may happen in future resulting into malicious usage of that particular card. Once predictive modeling is applied on this historical transactional data, any suspicious activity or uncharacteristic pattern is singled out and on basis of these predictions, precautionary actions can be initiated to mitigate and avoid any fraudulent events in near future.

Such predictive modeling helps to analyze the behavior of the customer’s transaction to reduce the number of false transactions. This big data technique analyses the transactional data and finds an unusual pattern in the payment procedure of the customers to predict a fraud way before it may actually occur.

With the help of credit card detection solution, banks can take steps further to ensure their business and cardholders are protected. Our solution helps to discover events that have abnormalities compared to a normal or typical behavior in the data to stop any kind of fraud.
