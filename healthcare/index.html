Tidycare - Predictive Healthcare App

Despite being no end to the debate that whether Machine Learning Solutions can assist more effectively in diagnosing a critical illness or can give prediction for such happenings with some acceptable accuracy, it’s no more a secret that research organization and healthcare establishments are actually have taken an acceptable stance to incorporate machine learning solutions in their overall methodology while working on symptoms of a critical disease to predict future course of these very symptoms. If nothing more, machine learning solutions are indeed aiding in the process and the day is not too far when these models will completely engrave themselves as most accurate mediums to assess the symptoms and present a diagnosis.

With historical medical data for an individual available in considerable volume, organizations concerned about stagnant state of predictive healthcare measures are slowly and gradually looking towards these machine learning solutions to put it on escalated mode and be of more help to patients and medical establishments alike. We, at Tidyquant, take pride in the fact for being an active contributor for the cause. Working in tandem with several research labs and healthcare establishments in multiple countries, we do have churned couple of such machine learning solutions which are helping medical practitioners to diagnose and offer precautionary medical aid to people carrying symptoms of terminal illnesses like cancer and heart ailments. Being an apt tool to assess and improve patient care, these predictive models are now being enquired by many more health establishments and research organizations.

The buck doesn’t stop at above. As more and more data become available to feed into a machine learning algorithm, the symptoms that can analyzed and predicted for a disease will cover plethora of life-threatening diseases and won’t be limited to heart ailments and different type of cancers. This will ultimately save precious human lives by taking preventive measures way before the advent of these life-threatening diseases.

When treatment of a disease can be aided by machines, using these very machines in identifying the symptoms and providing predictions before the actual occurrence of these diseases, is a step forward and should be considered this very way. At Tidyquant, we are continuously building healthcare solutions laden with artificial intelligence & machine learning algorithms to mine historical medical records to generate real-time disease preventive predictions. If you too are a healthcare establishment or a research organization and wants to be the contributor to the cause of improving predictive healthcare, drop us a line and we’ll speak on a future course of action.
